Introducing Splitwise

Splitwise is a mobile application that simplifies the process of sharing expenses and settling debts among friends, family, or colleagues. With Splitwise, users can create groups and track shared expenses, such as rent, bills, groceries, and travel costs. The app calculates how much each person owes and sends reminders when payments are due. It also allows users to add notes and attach receipts to expenses, making it easy to keep track of who paid for what.

Splitwise also offers a feature called "Simplify Debts," which helps users settle debts among friends or within a group. Rather than having each person pay the exact amount owed to each other, Simplify Debts calculates the minimum number of transactions required to settle all debts, which can save time and hassle.

Overall, Splitwise aims to make managing shared expenses and settling debts a stress-free experience.
